  • To create an event, log into your account, navigate to the dashboard, and click on "Create Event". Fill out the required details including event name, date, time, location, and description. You can also add optional details like images, tickets, and custom registration forms.

  • EventHub offers both free and premium plans. The free plan allows you to create basic events and manage RSVPs. Premium plans offer additional features like custom branding, advanced analytics, and priority support.

  • To join an organization, search for the organization in the "Organizations" section. Visit their profile and click "Join" or "Request to Join" depending on their membership settings. Some organizations may require approval before you can become a member.

  • Yes, EventHub supports ticket sales for events. When creating an event, you can set up different ticket types, prices, and quantities. We handle secure payment processing and provide tools to manage your ticket sales and attendee information.

  • EventHub provides multiple options to promote your event. You can share directly to social media, send email invitations to your contacts, feature your event in our directory, and create custom promotional codes. Premium users also have access to targeted advertising tools within our platform.
